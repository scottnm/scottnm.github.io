Fantasy Fudge

[ 2025-12-27 ]

Five or so years ago, I found a recipe for "Fantasy Fudge" online and the source link is now dead. My wife is very particular about fudge and has proclaimed this to be the recipe she remembers growing up, so I'm archiving it for reference. I suspect other online fudge recipes may be an exact copy of this or otherwise closely resemble this. But I prefer to have my own archive.

the recipe

Ingredients

Food

  • 3 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 5oz can of evaporated milk (not sweetened condensed milk)
  • 12 oz of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 7oz jar of marshmallow creme
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • (optional) 1 cup of chopped walnuts

Equipment

  • 9 inch square pan (or something else to let the fudge chill in)
  • aluminum foil
  • 3 qt pot or sauce pan
  • candy thermometer
  • rubber spatula

Instructions

  • Line 9-inch square pan with Reynolds Wrap® Aluminum Foil, with ends of foil extending over sides.
  • Bring sugar, butter, and evaporated milk to full rolling boil in 3 qt saucepan on medium heat, stirring constantly.
  • Cook until candy thermometer reaches 234°F, stirring constantly. Then, remove from heat
  • Add chocolate and marshmallow creme; stir until melted.
  • Add nuts and vanilla; mix well.
  • Pour into prepared pan and spread to cover bottom of pan.
  • Cool completely. Use foil handles to lift fudge from pan before cutting into 1-inch squares.
