NoSleep Podcast Favorites

[ 2026-01-25 ]

I'm a longtime listener of The NoSleep Podcast.

At time of writing, the show has over 20 seasons averaging 25 episodes a season. That's a lot of episodes!

This page is an index of all of my favorite episodes, unordered.

If you're looking to start listening, take any of these episodes for a spin!

S5E18: The "Soft White Damn" series

Something's haunting Danny. It follows him across the country at first asking then demanding to be let in. This is a four-part series and its worth following to the end! I love how every episode has its own "seasonal" flavor.

  1. Pt 1. S5E18: "Soft White Damn"
  2. Pt 2. S6E08: "Sure to Follow"
  3. Pt 3. S6E25: "Every Leaf is a Flower"
  4. Pt 4. S7E07: "The Earth, The Air, and You"
S23 Extras: Sleepless Decompositions Vol. 23 I'm usually not a huge fan of the "Sleepless Decompositions" series, but I really liked the voicemail framing device used in "After the Tone."
